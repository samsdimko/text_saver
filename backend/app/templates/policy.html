Privacy Policy for Text Saver

Text Saver ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Text Saver extension ("the Extension").

Information Collection and Use

While using our Extension, users can save and organize text-based content within the extension. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users, except for an email address if they choose to contact us with privacy-related inquiries.

Data Usage and Sharing

The saved texts are used solely for the purpose of organizing and displaying them to the user within the extension. We do not share the saved texts with any third parties or external services.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect the security of the saved texts. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users should check this policy periodically for any changes.
